For project 4, it is a chance let me bring the internet stuff to physical.
I decided to make a Instagram style photo frame and also allows you switch photos by swiping, just like swipe the photos on Instagram. And it also is a public booth, it also allows people to show any photos they want to show.

Ideation

Then

The character is a Schnauzer, my little daughter. I have choose 3 pictures and print them, then I stick 3 photos on black cardboard.

After that, I cut a piece of foam board and stuck it on cardboard to place and display the photo.

Design Progress

The hardest part for me is how to display the photo. At the beginning, I plan to seal the upper side and let it display only one photo.

Then I intend to use swiping way to switch the photo, just like Ins allows the user to swipe left and right to switch the photo.
